Changelog for 1.4.x:
  • Features:
    • Thumbnails of any image link are now supported.
    • You can now add emojis in your tweets with the Emoji Panel! (In composition pane)
    • Emojis are now in the party!
    • Added support of SoundCloud and Bandcamp for thumbnails/lightboxes
  • Bugfixes & Improvements:
    • Thumbnails should load faster as they are more accurate.
    • The whole extension should be more reliable and less error-prone.
    • Emojis are now (almost?) perfectly supported and shouldn't cause any problem.
    • Stability and performance improvements as minor bugfixes as well
